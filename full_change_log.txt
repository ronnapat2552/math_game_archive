Version Ruling Before Full Release
X.Y.Zz

X = Major Major | Break Backward Compat with Big New Feature
Y = Major Minor | Backward Compat with Big New Feature
Z = Minor Minor | Backward Compat with Small New Feature
z = Patch       | Fix Bug or Small Update or Quality of Life Update

0.4.00 < 0.4.01 < 0.4.02 < 0.4.03 < 0.4.10 < 0.4.11 < 0.4.20 < 0.4.30 < 0.50.0
